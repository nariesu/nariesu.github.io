Chalk Targets

New Release

Chief Jacquelyn "Jackie" Hall of the Rowley police along a surprisingly twisted path when subtle evidence indicates that the accidental death of a local woman she has known for years may be something more disturbing.

When a young investigator named Marcia Grieves is assigned to the case by the Essex County DA's office, Jackie is at first unsure of how much help she will be, but she soon comes to respect and welcome the impartial second set of eyes as unsettling secrets are discovered regarding the victim, her husband, and their step-kids.

Ken Blaisdell grew up in the small rural town of Rowley, located on Massachusetts' north shore. He attended high school in nearby Newburyport. He now resides in Gilbert, Arizona where his close-knit cul-de-sac community. Ken's writing blends his personal experiences with meticulous research to create believable characters and situations that leave the reader wondering where reality stops and the fiction begins.
