Smashword

Smashwords, along with their partner Draft2Digital, is the word's largest independent distributor of digitally formatted books. Supported formats include Kindle, Apple Books, Nook, Kobo, Google Play, and more.

When you buy an e-book from Smashwords.com an "epub" version of the book is sent as an attachment to the email address you used to open your Smashwords account. Open that email and forward the entire thing to the email address of your Kindle device. (If you don't know what it is, see below.) There is no need to include a subject. Within a minute or so, the book will appear in your Kindle reader.

Finding Your Kindle's email address


If you don't know your device's email address (and few people do) you can find it either on your Kindle device or through your Amazon account on your computer.


On Amazon through your computer


  1. Go to: www.Amazon.com/myk
  2. From the "Manage Your Content and Devices" tool bar near the top, select "Devices"
  3. If you have more than one Kindle device registered, a box will appear noting how many there are. If so, click on that box to show them
  4. Click on the name of the device you want to send the new e-book to. Information about that device will pop up, including its default email address (e.g. NameVj123@kindle.com)
  5. Copy the email address and use it to forward the entire email you received from Smashwords


On your Kindle Device


  1. On your home page, tap the three vertical dots at the top right to open the options dropdown
  2. Tap "Settings"
  3. Tap "Your Account"
  4. At the bottom will be "Send-to-Kindle Email." At the end of that paragraph is the email address (e.g. NameVj123@kindle.com)

NOTE: If you wish to change the address to something easier to remember you must do that through your computer, as outlined above
